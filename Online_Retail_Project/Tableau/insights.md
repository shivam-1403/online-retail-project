# 📊 Tableau Dashboard Insights

## 🧾 Overview
This dashboard analyzes online retail sales to identify trends in revenue, customer behavior, product performance, and regional contributions.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Growth Trend
- Revenue shows a clear upward trajectory over time, especially in the final months.
- Indicates seasonal demand or improved sales performance toward year-end.

### 2. Order Volume Pattern
- Order count follows a similar trend as revenue but with fluctuations.
- Suggests that revenue growth is driven by both increased orders and higher order values.

### 3. Average Order Value (AOV)
- AOV remains relatively stable throughout the period.
- Slight increase in later months indicates customers are spending more per transaction.

---

## 🛍️ Product Performance

### 4. Revenue Contribution
- A small group of products contributes significantly to total revenue.
- Indicates a **Pareto effect (80/20 rule)** in product sales.

### 5. Quantity vs Revenue Gap
- Some products have high quantity but lower revenue contribution.
- Suggests presence of **low-priced, high-volume items**.

---

## 🌍 Geographic Insights

### 6. Country-wise Revenue Distribution
- Revenue is concentrated in a few key countries.
- Removing dominant regions (like the UK) helps reveal meaningful comparisons among others.

### 7. Expansion Opportunity
- Countries with moderate performance may present growth opportunities with targeted strategies.

---

## 🚚 Order Status Analysis

### 8. Delivery Performance
- ~84% of orders are successfully delivered.

### 9. Cancellation Rate
- ~16% cancellation rate is significant and may impact revenue.
- Indicates potential issues in logistics, stock availability, or customer intent.

---

## 🎯 Business Recommendations

- Focus on high-revenue products for marketing and inventory optimization
- Investigate reasons behind cancellations to reduce loss
- Explore growth opportunities in underperforming regions
- Monitor AOV trends to identify upselling opportunities

---

## 📌 Conclusion
The business demonstrates strong revenue growth and consistent customer behavior. Strategic improvements in product focus and order fulfillment can further enhance performance.
