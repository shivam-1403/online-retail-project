# 📊 Online Retail Sales Dashboard – Key Insights

## 💰 Overall Business Performance
- Total revenue generated is **£10,644,560.42** from **24,447 orders**.
- The dataset includes **4,373 unique customers**.
- The **Average Order Value (AOV)** is approximately **£435.41**, indicating moderate spending per transaction.
- The **cancellation rate is 15.69%**, which is relatively high and suggests potential operational or product-related issues.

---

## 📦 Product Performance Insights
- **DOTCOM POSTAGE** generates the highest revenue (**£206,248.77**), followed by **REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER (£174,484.74)**.
- **SMALL POPCORN HOLDER** is the most sold product (**56,450 units**), followed by **WORLD WAR 2 GLIDERS ASSTD DESIGNS (53,847 units)**.
- There is a clear distinction between:
  - High-revenue products 💰
  - High-volume products 📦  
  indicating that some products sell in large quantities but contribute less to overall revenue.

---

## 📈 Sales & Orders Trend
- Monthly orders show an overall **increasing trend**, with minor fluctuations.
- **November 2011** is the most active month with **3,387 orders**, indicating strong end-of-year demand.
- Revenue trend follows a similar pattern, confirming that **higher order volume drives revenue growth**.

---

## 🛒 Customer Spending Behavior (AOV)
- Monthly AOV remains relatively **stable throughout the year**.
- The highest AOV is observed in **January 2011 (£627.37)**.
- A slight decline is observed in later months, with **November 2011 at £512.39**, suggesting:
  - Increased order volume but slightly lower spending per order.

---

## 🚚 Order Fulfillment & Cancellation
- Approximately **95% of orders are successfully delivered**, while **5% are cancelled**.
- Despite a relatively high cancellation rate (~15.69% at line level), most orders are fulfilled successfully.
- This indicates:
  - Strong delivery performance  
  - But potential issues in specific products or transactions leading to cancellations  

---

## ⚠️ Data Considerations
- **December data was excluded** from trend analysis due to incomplete records.
- A significant portion of customer data is missing (`CustomerID`), limiting detailed customer-level insights.
- Revenue includes non-product entries such as **POSTAGE**, indicating additional operational charges.

---

## 🧾 Final Summary
The business demonstrates strong overall performance with increasing order volume and stable customer spending patterns. Revenue growth is primarily driven by increased transactions toward the end of the year. However, the presence of high cancellation rates and missing customer data highlights areas for potential operational improvements and deeper analysis.
